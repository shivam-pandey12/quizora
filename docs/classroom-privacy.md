# Classroom Privacy

Classroom data is private by default.

## Protected Data

- Class membership.
- Student emails.
- Assignment submissions.
- Attempt answer snapshots.
- Class-only live rooms.
- Creator-only draft quizzes.

## Student-Safe Data

Students may see:

- Class title and teacher name for classes they joined.
- Published assignments in their classes.
- Their own submissions.
- Safe class leaderboard rows with display name, score, completed count, and accuracy.

Students may not see:

- Other students' detailed submissions.
- Other students' answer snapshots.
- Teacher/admin notes.
- Private classes they have not joined.

## SEO

All classroom, creator, and assignment routes are noindex and omitted from the sitemap.
