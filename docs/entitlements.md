# Entitlements

Entitlements unlock Quizora paid access. The effective plan is the highest-priority active entitlement that has not expired.

Priority:

1. Classroom
2. Creator
3. Plus
4. Free fallback

Same-plan purchases extend expiry. Higher-plan purchases become the effective plan while lower active entitlements can remain in history.

Statuses:

- `active`
- `expired`
- `cancelled`
- `revoked`
- `pending`

Manual grants and revokes are admin-only and should create billing audit logs.
